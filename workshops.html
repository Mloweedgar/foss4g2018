DESCRIPTION

Workshops represent the practical approach for participants. We know that many of the participants comes to increase their practical knowledge about open source GIS tools and methods. In our call for workshops we want to build up a list of prospective workshops. This includes workshops proposed by the organizational committee as well as those coming from others. Workshops should showcase current and emerging FOSSGIS technologies, foster skills development in the open geospatial domain and provide a direct interaction between our highly skilled OSGEO community and the public at large who want to acquire new skills.
